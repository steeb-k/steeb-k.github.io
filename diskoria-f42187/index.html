Diskoria

Storage health, sector scans, and real-world benchmarks — in one portable Windows app.

Custom CPU-rendered UI. No GPU drivers, no OpenGL, no installer required — it even runs inside Windows recovery.

  1. Drive Health

    Know your drive's health at a glance

    SMART, NVMe, and UFS diagnostics read straight from the drive — temperature, wear level, power-on hours, data written, and media errors, all in one live panel, with a temperature history chart spanning the last hour to seven days.

  2. Sector Read Test

    Scan every sector, completely read-only

    A non-destructive surface scan reads the entire disk sequentially and maps each block as good, slow, or bad — watch the heat-map fill in block by block while bad sectors are isolated to pinpoint the trouble.

  3. Verdict

    An answer you can't misread

    When the scan finishes, Diskoria gives you a single, unmistakable verdict — a full-screen PASS or FAIL. No squinting at logs to decide whether the drive is okay.

  4. Performance Chart

    See speed across the whole platter

    Switch from the sector map to the performance chart to see throughput plotted against position on the disk — slow zones and degradation jump right out, with average speed, bad sectors, and timing pinned below.

  5. Reports

    Export a clean, shareable report

    Generate a polished health report in one click — model, serial, bus, media, capacity, and every vital, topped with the performance graph. Ready to hand to a client, a buyer, or your future self.

  6. Deep Dive

    Zoom in on every detail

    Pop any chart out to full screen, scroll to zoom, and drag to pan — inspect each dip in throughput down to the gigabyte, with the raw samples scattered behind the smoothed line so averaging hides nothing.

  7. Benchmark

    Measure real-world speed

    A file-based benchmark measures sequential and random 4K read/write performance, so you see how the drive actually behaves — not what the box claims — in four big, legible numbers.
