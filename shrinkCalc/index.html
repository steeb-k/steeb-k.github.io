How are these results calculated?

Calculation Formulas

XY Shrinkage

XY = ( Xtop + Xmiddle + Xbottom 3 + Ytop + Ymiddle + Ybottom 3 ) 40 × CurrentXY%

Where:
- X and Y values are averaged separately
- The two averages are then averaged together
- Divided by 20 (shown as ÷40 in the formula)
- Multiplied by your current XY shrinkage percentage

Z Shrinkage

Z = Ztop + Zmiddle + Zbottom 60 × CurrentZ%

Where:
- Z values are averaged
- Divided by 20 (shown as ÷60 in the formula)
- Multiplied by your current Z shrinkage percentage

How to Use This Tool

Here's a brief guide on using the Shrinkage Calculator:

  1. Enter your measurement values for each axis (X, Y, Z) across the three positions (Top, Middle, Bottom)
  2. Set your current shrinkage percentages (default is 100%)
  3. Click the "Calculate" button to process the data
  4. View your adjusted XY Shrinkage and Z Shrinkage results
  5. The calculator averages each axis across positions and divides by 20
  6. Results are then adjusted by your current shrinkage percentages

For best results, ensure all fields are filled with accurate measurements.
